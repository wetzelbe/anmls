ANMLS

ANMLS are NFTs, that have additional logic to them. You can mate two ANMLS and will get their child.
All ANMLS have DNA, that determines what they look like. When breeding, the DNA of the parents is combined to create the DNA of the child.
This Process keeps the parts of the DNA that is shared between parents and will pseudo-randomly fill the rest in.
Connect your MetaMask-Wallet to get started.

Technology

ANMLS are ERC721-compliant Tokens. They also use the ERC721Metadata and ERC721Enumerable-Extentions.
Currently the dapp is deployed on the Ropsten Testnet.
